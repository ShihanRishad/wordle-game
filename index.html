What is Wordle?

Wordle is a word game where you have to guess a five-letter word in six tries. Each guess must be a valid five-letter word. After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.


How to Play

1. Enter a five-letter word and press .
2. The tiles will change color to give you hints:
- Green: Correct letter in the correct spot.
- Yellow: Correct letter in the wrong spot.
- Gray: Letter is not in the word.
3. Use these hints to guess the word within six tries.
